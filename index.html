About LACE

The mission of Lexington Advocates for Civic Engagement (LACE) is to promote civic engagement, encourage public participation in local governance, and advocate for community-driven policy initiatives in Lexington, Massachusetts.

LACE aims to educate, mobilize, and empower residents through civic forums, voter education campaigns, advocacy for local policy changes, and grassroots organizing. These efforts are designed to foster informed decision-making and enhance public involvement in democratic processes.
