About LACE

Lexington Advocates for Civic Engagement (LACE) is a nonprofit organization dedicated to promoting civic participation, local government engagement, and community-driven policy initiatives in Lexington, MA. Our mission is to educate, mobilize, and empower residents to take an active role in shaping policies that enhance civic life and democratic engagement. We achieve this through public forums, voter education, issue advocacy, and grassroots organizing, all in support of the public good
